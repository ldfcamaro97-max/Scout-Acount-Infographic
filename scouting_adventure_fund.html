💰 Earn Your Way with a "Scout Account"

A "Scout Account" is like a personal bank account for your Scouting journey. It's funded by your own efforts in council-approved fundraisers and can be used to pay for camp, gear, uniforms, and more. Here are the primary ways to build your account:

🍿

Popcorn Sales

A council-wide tradition! A generous portion of every sale you make goes directly into your Scout Account to fund big adventures.

💳

Camp Card Sales

Earn a massive 50% commission! For every $10 card you sell, $5 goes directly into your personal account.
